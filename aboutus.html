What is IrmaList?

IrmaList (stands for: Interactive Reasonable Marketplace Agency) is an advertisement website which puts extreme emphasis on user ability to customize their postings to our website. This is due to the belief shared by our team that to present your offer properly is a vital skill, especially nowadays when other platforms of similar purpose are flooded with low quality ads that do not properly reflect the product being sold. Our mission statement is to combat the stigma towards second hand purchases by offering the tools to professionally showcase one's offerings.
